FAQs

What’s a little Sp_ACE?

A little Sp_ACE is just that; it’s a little Sp_ACE on the internet. They’re new and you’ll see more and more of the people and brands you know and love making use of their official little Sp_ACE on the internet.

Why are little Sp_ACES empty?

All little Sp_ACES start empty but they don’t stay that way for long because once a year, at a time of their choosing, the Sp_ACE creator ( brand / person ) drops something unique into their Sp_ACE to fill it up.

How do I access their little Sp_ACE?

Only co-owners of someone’s little Sp_ACE can access the mystery drop. Become a co-owner by finding a Sp_ACE you like, checking out the co-owner price and then pay by card to become a co-owner.

I’m a co-owner, what next?

You’ll receive your co-owner code by email and you’ll enter it on drop day to access the little Sp_ACE you co-own. Only the Sp_ACE creator knows when they’ll drop so watch their socials for hints and teases.

How many co-owners will there be?

This is entirely up to the creator and largely depends on what they’re intending to drop into their little Sp_ACE to fill it up. A digital artist who is dropping new art will likely choose less co-owners that a music artist who plans to drop new music in a surprise irl performance.

So the drop date and drop Is a mystery?

Yep, that’s all part of the fun.
